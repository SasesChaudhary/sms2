Stationery Management System

Stationery management system is a web-based system that is designed for the stores that deals with sells and purchase of stationery products.
This system further helps in the maintenance of stocks. This system is designed to meet the needs for storekeeper to be able to manage their accounts in a way that it will be easier to keep stock records.
Maintaining the account details and sales record is the main aim of this project. The Stationery Management System is used for stationery purposes and it helps to maintain the regular transaction records on the desktop.
